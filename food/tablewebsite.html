Buffalo Chicken Bowls with Cauliflower Garlic Rice
The ultimate meal prep! Spicy buffalo chicken, a fluffy rice and cauliflower rice combo, fresh tomatoes and cucumbers, and a dollop of creamy dill dip.
Ingredients:
4 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 large head of cauliflower
3-4 garlic cloves, sliced
1/2 cup thickly sliced green onion, both green and white parts
1 cup + 1/4 cup chicken stock
1 tsp poultry seasoning
3/4 cup Franks Red Hot Sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce or Gluten-Free Worcestershire Sauce
2 tsp Green Tabasco sauce or other green hot sauce
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons Golden Monkfruit Sweetener, or sweetener of your choice
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/3 cup crumbled blue cheese
Salt and fresh ground black pepper to season cauliflower
